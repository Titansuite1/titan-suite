The Titan Workflow

V5 tells you when to start watching. V6 tells you when execution improves. V7 tells you when risk is rising. Unlimited combines all three.

Best Timeframes

Titan was built for swing and macro-style decision making. Weekly, monthly, 6-month, and long-term timeframes generally provide the cleanest cycle context.

Markets

Titan can be applied to crypto, equities, ETFs, and macro charts. Crypto often responds more aggressively to cycle and volatility conditions, while equities tend to respond more gradually to trend and macro conditions.
